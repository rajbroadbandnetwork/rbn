FAQ

Do you have installation charges?

Basic self-install is free with the Essential plan. Professional installation is free on Premium; charges may apply for complex setups.

Is there a data cap?

No — our standard plans are unlimited. Fair usage policy applies in extreme cases (e.g., sustained terabyte-level usage).

What is the contract length?

Monthly rolling plans are available. Discounts for 6- or 12-month prepay options.

Business SLAs?

Business plans include SLA options with guaranteed uptime and faster incident response times. Contact sales for details.
